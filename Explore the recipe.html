Owl Cake

Ingredients

  • Cake mix (your choice of flavor)
  • Buttercream frosting (chocolate and vanilla)
  • Chocolate chips or candy for decoration (for eyes and beak)
  • Round cake pans

Recipe

  1. Prepare the cake mix according to the instructions on the package.
  2. Bake the cakes in round cake pans.
  4. Let the cakes cool completely.
  5. Once cooled, place one round cake on a serving plate or cake stand.
  6. Use chocolate buttercream frosting to frost the entire cake.
  7. Place the second round cake on top of the first one.
  9. Use vanilla buttercream frosting to frost the entire cake, leaving a small area in the center unfrosted for the owl's face.
  10. Use chocolate chips or candy to create the owl's eyes on the unfrosted area.
  11. Use a small amount of frosting to attach a chocolate chip or candy as the beak just below the eyes.
  12. Use additional frosting to create feather patterns around the owl's face.
  13. Serve and enjoy your owl cake!

Bournville Brownies

Ingredients

  • 200g Bournville chocolate
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 75g all-purpose flour
  • A pinch of salt

Recipe

  1. Melt 200g Bournville chocolate and 150g unsalted butter together. Let it cool slightly..
  2. Whisk 200g granulated sugar, 3 large eggs, and 1 tsp vanilla extract. Gradually add the melted chocolate mixture..
  3. Fold in 75g all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan and bake at 180Â°C for 25-30 minutes..

Milk Tray

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream, chilled
  • Milk Tray chocolates

Recipe

  1. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Fold whipped cream into condensed milk..
  3. Break Milk Tray chocolates into pieces and fold into mixture..
  4. Transfer to a container, freeze for 6 hours or overnight.
  5. Enjoy your homemade Milk Tray ice cream!
